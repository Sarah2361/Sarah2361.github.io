Sarah Cornehls

Author | Musician

Sarah Cornehls is an emerging author from Melbourne, Australia. She has a background in the Arts (specifically Music and English literature). Her genres consist of Fiction (contemporary and urban fiction) and non fiction (usually memoir). Sarah combines the similarities with Music and Literature in her works, and makes use of music termiology in her writing.

Sarah enjoys capturing real life experiences and turning them into fictional stories. She enjoys capturing the essence of human nature and how us as humans deal and respond to different situations. Sarah challenges herself to dip her finger into every genre possible (from fiction, to manga and comics, to non fiction, to interactive fiction) and loves experiencing the different ways of storytelling. From a young age she has always loved reading and writing, and creating her own stories.
