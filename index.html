Sarah Cornehls

Author | Musician

Sarah Cornehls is an emerging author from Melbourne, Australia. She has a background in the Arts (specifically Music and English literature). Her genres consist of Fiction (contemporary and urban fiction) and non fiction (usually memoir). award-winning author of fiction and nonfiction, known for her evocative storytelling and deep engagement with the human condition. Her work has been published in numerous international journals and anthologies.

With a background in journalism and literature, Jane blends research with creativity to produce compelling narratives. She is passionate about mentoring emerging writers and often appears at festivals and workshops around the globe.
